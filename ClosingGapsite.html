Closing the Gap Mobile Build

Overview

Closing the Gap is a program in central Florida that is bridges the educational gap between young boys and girls in public schools.


Problem

The program has seen plenty of success and garnered attention by investors, but there is currently no site for the program. They also want to expand to service the rest of Florida.


Solution

Me and my team coded a mobile site for the program using hifi prototypes as a foundation. We wanted to fulfill our stakeholders main goal of getting more program applicants and give our users the information and resources they needed about Closing the Gap.
