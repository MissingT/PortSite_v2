NSA Responsive Redesign

Overview

The NSA leads the U.S. Government in cryptology which encompasses both cybersecurity and signals intelligence.


Problem

Their site is loaded with content and an unorganized information hierarchy that makes it hard to navigate the site. The visual presentation of the site also does not make it seem related with the government.


Solution

My main goal when redesigning the site was to simplify the information hierarchy on both the desktop and mobile versions for easier navigation. I also updated the site's visual design with their brand guidelines in order to give our user's more confidence in the legitimacy of the site.
