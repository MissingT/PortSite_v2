S.O.A.K. Responsive Redesign

Overview

S.O.A.K provides resources and direct support to enable families with autistic children to connect and assist each other through social support programs.


Problem

S.O.A.K. had almost no digital presence aside from their popular Facebook group. Their website was barren and had almost no functionality for potential users.


Solution

Me and my team created a responsive site for S.O.A.K. that would still give access to the already popular Facebook group, but also give our user's the option to learn more about autism, have access to a library of resources, get involved with S.O.A.K. and donate to the organization.
